Description of the Landscaping course

Unlock your creativity and transform outdoor spaces with our hands-on Landscaping Course.
This practical programme is designed to equip learners with essential skills in garden design, plant selection, soil preparation, turf installation, hardscaping, and sustainable landscaping practices.
Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your current skills, this course blends theory with real-world projects to help you plan, design, and maintain beautiful, functional landscapes. Through expert instruction and practical exercises, you'll gain confidence in using landscaping tools, understanding seasonal planting,
and creating outdoor environments that are both aesthetically pleasing and environmentally friendly. Perfect for aspiring landscapers, garden enthusiasts, or anyone passionate about working with nature.

You will be qualified to deal with:

  • Indigenous and exotic plants and trees fixed structures(foundations, statues, benches, tables, bult-in braai)
  • Balancing of plants and trees in a garden
  • Aesthetic of plant shapes and colours
  • Garden layout

Course fees: R1500
