First Aid 🚑

Description of the First aid course

Learn essential life-saving skills in our comprehensive First Aid Course, designed to equip you with the knowledge and confidence to respond effectively in emergency situations.
This practical training covers a wide range of topics including CPR, wound care, choking, burns, fractures, and more.
Whether you're a parent, teacher, employee, or simply someone who wants to be prepared, this course offers hands-on experience and clear guidance from certified instructors.
Upon completion, participants will receive a First Aid Certificate, empowering them to make a difference when it matters most.

You will be qualified to deal with:

  • Wounds and Bleeding
  • Burns and Fractures
  • Emergency scene management
  • Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR)
  • Respiratory distress e.g Chocking, blocking airway
