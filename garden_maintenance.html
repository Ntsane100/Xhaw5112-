Description of the Garden Maintenance course

Unlock the secrets to a beautiful and thriving garden with our hands-on Garden Maintenance Course.
Designed for beginners and gardening enthusiasts alike, this course covers essential skills to help you care for and maintain gardens of all sizes.
You will learn practical techniques in soil preparation, planting, pruning, weeding, pest control, seasonal care, and lawn maintenance.
The course combines theory with guided outdoor sessions, giving you real-world experience and confidence to manage residential or commercial green spaces.

You will be qualified to deal with:

  • Water restrictions and the watering requirements of indigeneous and exotic palnts
  • Pruning and propagation of plants
  • Planting techniques for different plants types
