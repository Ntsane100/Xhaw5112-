Description of the Childminding course

This comprehensive Childminding Course is designed for individuals who are passionate about working with children and want to build a rewarding career in early childhood care.
Whether you're starting your journey or looking to enhance your existing skills, this course equips you with the essential knowledge and practical tools needed to provide safe, nurturing, and educational environments for young children.
Throughout the course, you will explore key topics such as child development, health and safety, play-based learning, safeguarding practices, and effective communication with both children and parents.
You will also gain valuable insights into setting up and managing a childminding business, including legal responsibilities and professional standards

You will be qualified to deal with:

  • Birth to six month old baby needs
  • Seven month to one year old needs
  • Toddler needs
  • Educational toys
