Description of the Sewing course

Unlock your creativity and gain practical, hands-on experience in sewing with our comprehensive Sewing Skills Training Course.
Designed for beginners and hobbyists, this course covers everything from the basics of hand stitching and machine operation to fabric selection, pattern reading, and garment construction.
Participants will learn essential techniques such as hemming, seam finishing, zipper installation, and more all while creating their own personalized sewing projects.
Whether you want to repair clothes, start a small business, or simply enjoy a fulfilling new hobby, this course equips you with the skills and confidence to bring your ideas to life.

You will be qualified to deal with:

  • Types of stitches
  • Threading a sewing machine, sewing buttons, zips, hems and seams
  • Alterations
  • Designing and sewing new garments

Course fees: R1500
