Description of the Life Skills course

The Life Skills Course is designed to equip individuals with essential tools and practical knowledge to navigate everyday life confidently and successfully.
Through interactive sessions and real-life scenarios, participants will develop critical thinking, communication, decision-making, emotional intelligence, and problem-solving skills.
The course also covers topics such as financial literacy, time management, personal hygiene, relationships, goal setting, and self-awareness.
Whether you're preparing for independence, employment, or personal growth, this course provides a strong foundation for a more empowered and fulfilling life.

You will be qualified to deal with:

  • Open a bank account
  • Basic labour law
  • Basic reading and writing literacy
  • Basic numeric literacy

Course fees: R1500
