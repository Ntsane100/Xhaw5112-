Description of the Cooking course

Unlock your culinary potential with this hands-on cooking course designed for beginners and aspiring home chefs!
Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to refine your kitchen skills, this course will guide you through essential techniques, classic recipes, and creative meal ideas.
Learn everything from knife skills and food safety to baking, seasoning, and plating like a pro. By the end, you will be able to prepare delicious, well-balanced meals with confidence and maybe even impress your family and friends!

You will be qualified to deal with:

  • Nutritional requirements for a healthy body
  • Types of protein, carbohydrates and vegatables
  • Planning meals
  • Tasty and nutritious recipes
